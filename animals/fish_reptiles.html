  • Fish & Reptiles

Reptiles are hard but rewarding to own. From Lizards to Terrapins, there are hundreds of variety of reptiles. Sadly many of these are not allowed in New Zealand as they could damage our fragile ecosystem if they are released into the wild. The upkeep of reptiles nearly always involves a heat lamp due to them being cold blooded. That often means that during winter they don't move all too much to preserve heat, so if you need an active pet there may be a better choice.

Fish are divided into two main categories, Coldwater and Tropical. Coldwater fish don't require anything but an air bubbler and a filter. At mimimum this can be as low as $30, or as high as $500 in a standard size tank. We sell a variety of tanks in multiple price ranges for the many budgets and expertise that are looking for equipment.
